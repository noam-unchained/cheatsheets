JOHN THE RIPPER — QUICK REF

Requires: a hash file (often produced by a *2john extractor)

1.  Extract a hash (*2john):
    zip2john secret.zip > zip.hash
    rar2john secret.rar > rar.hash
    pdf2john.pl doc.pdf > pdf.hash
    ssh2john id_rsa > ssh.hash
    keepass2john db.kdbx > kp.hash
    office2john report.docx > office.hash
    unshadow /etc/passwd /etc/shadow > linux.hash

2.  Wordlist (auto-detect):
    john --wordlist=/usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt <hashfile>
    john --show <hashfile>
    # force format:
    john --format=krb5tgs --wordlist=rockyou.txt <hashfile>

3.  Rules / incremental / mask:
    john --wordlist=rockyou.txt --rules <hashfile>
    john --incremental <hashfile>
    john --mask='?u?l?l?l?l?d?d' <hashfile>

4.  Manage:
    john --status
    john --restore
    john --users=<user1,user2> <hashfile>
    # cracked stored in ~/.john/john.pot

5.  End-to-end:
    ssh2john id_rsa > h && john --wordlist=rockyou.txt h      # then ssh -i id_rsa
    keepass2john db.kdbx > h && john --wordlist=rockyou.txt h
